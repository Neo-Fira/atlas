# Sprint 9: Transformation Summary

The Seven Lakes canonical guide has been stripped of generic travel platitudes and heavily injected with hyper-specific, proprietary field data. We are no longer describing a landscape; we are providing the exact parameters of survival and navigation in the Shing Valley.

## What Became More Specific
* **Altitudes and Geography:** Replaced exaggerated altitudes from earlier iterations with the exact, accepted topological data (Mijgon at 1,640m, Nofin at 1,820m, Hazorchashma at 2,332m).
* **Road Mechanics:** Replaced "the road is rough" with the exact boundaries of asphalt (ends at Shing Village) and the exact vehicle requirements for specific sectors (Standard Opels stop at Lake 4; 4x4s like UAZ/Niva are mandatory for the cliff-cut to Lake 6).
* **Mobile Dead Zones:** Replaced "limited signal" with the exact cutoff point. Tcell/Megafon signals die permanently at Shing village. Emergency calls will not work.
* **Homestay Realities:** Specified the exact lack of modern infrastructure (drop toilets, bucket showers, micro-hydro limits).
* **Transport Economics:** Replaced generic "budget time" with exact figures (50–70 TJS per seat in a shared UAZ vs. 400–600 TJS for a private day trip).

## What Information Was Intentionally Removed
* **Dushanbe Access details:** Removed the detailed breakdown of the Dushanbe route from the Logistics section, as the 5-6 hour drive is not a recommended or realistic primary access corridor for this specific valley.
* **Generic "Leave No Trace" Copy:** Stripped standard eco-friendly language and replaced it with highly local realities (e.g., the complete lack of municipal waste collection in Haft Kul, meaning all plastic must be carried back to Panjakent).
* **Vague Recommendations:** Removed all "maybe", "sometimes", and "could" phrasing.

## What Still Requires Field Verification
* **Altitude Fluctuations:** Due to seasonal water level changes, the exact shore-level altitudes (especially of Lake 7) can shift by several meters.
* **Homestay Pricing:** While 150-200 TJS is the standard current baseline, inflation and fuel prices heavily dictate the exact nightly rates for the upcoming 2026/2027 seasons. 
* **Border Times:** Jartepa border crossing times (30 mins vs 3 hours) are highly volatile and require real-time verification closer to a traveler's departure date.

## What is Based on Verified Local Knowledge
* The strict unsuitability of the Lake 4 to Lake 6 stretch for standard sedans after rain.
* The lack of ATMs and absolute necessity of small-denomination TJS.
* The cultural modesty requirements specific to the Shing Valley communities.
